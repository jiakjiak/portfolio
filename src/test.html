THE SKY'S THE LIMIT

about

Jia is a current RISE scholarship recipient and preparing to make a career transition into Project Management in the tech world. Known for her airline operations expertise, she has over eight years of experience in the aviation industry and has worked for Fortune 500 companies, including Boeing and Emirates. Her background in leadership and customer experience spans five countries: Singapore, Greenland, Canada, United Arab Emirates and the United States.

Jia is fueled by her passion to improve service delivery and streamline operational processes. She considers herself a "curious learner", eager to build and strengthen her technical education in Project Management and frontend development through continued coding, coursework, and professional development.

Jia's hunger for knowledge and pursuit of excellence has contributed to her most recent success at Boeing. She won second place in a digital hackathon in a cross-collaboration with her team. She also improved her department's service excellence awareness by delivering knowledge sharing sessions for her team. Jia believes empathy in the workplace is key to success, a tenet she lives by while volunteering as a Crisis Counsellor. She enjoys travelling, experiencing new cultures, and supporting causes for women with recurrent pregnancy loss.

Jia is preparing to return to the workforce and make an impact. Reach out to her using the contact form below to connect!
